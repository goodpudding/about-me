All about Trey Young, the cat guy

Short Bio

Born into a military family in South Carolina, I mostly grew up in Alaska with my Moms side of the family. I moved quite a bit, so I constantly had to learn how to adapt to new situations. I have lived in Alaska, Arkansas, Indiana, Georgia, California and I moved to Washington state in 2019.

Education History

I graduated in 2008 at Mountain Home High School in 2008. I took courses here and there about computer networking and IT, but it wasn't until I got out of the military that I started taking college more serious. I attend the Art Institute in San Francisco 2015 for Video Game Programming and Desing, but I took a film elective and thought that was more fun, so switched my major. In the winter of 2018, the school shut down and I transferred to the Seattle campus at the beginning of 2019. 2 months later, the Art Institute of Seattle shut its doors permanetly. I was able to transfer my credits to the Seattle Film Institute and finish my Bachelors of Arts in Digital Media Productions in the fall of 2019.

Job experience

I've always had a pretty good work ethic. My younger years, I did the typical mow yard, rake leaves, etc. My mom ran a daycare in my early teens and I took a babysitters course and started babysitting for my moms clients. My first official job was TacoBell and I did fast food/customer service until I finally got fed up with it and decided to join the military. I served 4 years in the US Army as a satellite communications specialist. It was a great experience and I learned a lot, but did not enjoy the military life style. I got out and used the GI bill to go to film school. When I graduated film school, I couldn't find any work in the field and I started working with a company that builds catios in the Seattle area. After working with them for 2 years, I decided to become a general contractor and have been running my general contracting business.
